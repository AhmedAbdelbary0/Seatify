Frequently Asked Questions

How does Seatify help me manage my venue?

Seatify lets you design the layout and share a QR code for instant bookings. This simplifies the seating process for small-scale venues and rooms.

Can I customize seat layouts for different events?

Yes! You can design each layout independently per event.

Do my attendees need to install an app to book?

No installation needed — Seatify works directly through QR-based web booking.

Can I manage multiple venues under one account?

Yes, one account can manage multiple halls or rooms easily.

How can I track bookings?

You can view and export booking data instantly through your dashboard.
